SCMU 17

The Speed Cubing Mumbai Unlimited (SCMU) KJSCE Open 2017 was held as a pre-event to flag off Abhiyantriki 2017. The event saw participation from participants aged 6 to those in their 30s, including Mr. Mats Valk, one of the world’s best cubers and also the holder of numerous World Records. Events like 2x2, 3x3, Clock, 6x6, 7x7, Pyraminx, 3x3 Blindfold (BLD), 3x3 One-handed (OH), Multiple Blindfolded, Megaminx, Skewb, and Feet were held. A lot of records were made and at the same time a lot were broken again. All in all the event was a huge success and laid a solid foundation for Abhiyantriki 2017.
